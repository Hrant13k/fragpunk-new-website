Jaguar

Assassin

A master of stealth and precision strikes, Jaguar excels at flanking the enemy team and creating chaos behind their lines. Equipped with state-of-the-art cloaking tech, she is the ultimate predator of the arena.

Abilities

Predator Cloak

Become temporarily invisible to enemy radar and sight. Attacking breaks the cloak.

Pounce Dash

Perform a quick multidirectional dash to dodge incoming fire or close the gap.

Crippling Dart

Fire a specialized dart that slows the target and reveals their position to the team.

Apex Strike (Ultimate)

Lock onto all visible enemies within a radius and unleash a devastating flurry of guaranteed critical hits.
